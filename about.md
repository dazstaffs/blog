## About Darren Stafford

Darren Stafford is an Application Delivery Manager with extensive experience across software engineering, delivery leadership, and enterprise technology transformation within the London Market insurance sector.

He began his career in IT support before transitioning into software development and later into delivery management, where he now leads cross-functional engineering teams responsible for building and modernising critical insurance platforms. Darren has delivered large-scale initiatives including policy administration system integrations, cloud platform adoption, and the modernisation of legacy in-house applications.

His work focuses on improving the way technology teams deliver value by combining **Agile delivery practices, DevOps principles, and cloud-native technologies**. He has successfully led projects integrating third-party platforms such as Moody’s IRP and the Sapien IDIT suite, while also overseeing the development of custom APIs and internal tools to support underwriting, actuarial, and claims operations.

Alongside delivery leadership, Darren has driven operational improvements including cloud cost optimisation, technical debt reduction, and the implementation of data-driven delivery metrics using Power BI. He is also passionate about mentoring engineers, improving team culture, and introducing new tools and practices that help organisations modernise their ways of working.

Darren holds certifications in Agile Project Management, PRINCE2, ITIL, Microsoft Azure (AZ-900), Azure AI Fundamentals (AI-900), and the Chartered Insurance Institute’s London Market Insurance qualifications (LM1 & LM2). He is currently studying for the Microsoft Azure Developer Associate certification (AZ-204).
